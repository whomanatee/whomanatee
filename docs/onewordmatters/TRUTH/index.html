TRUTH

A DECLARATION

by Mohammad J. D'Bahama
Sun, Jul 11, 2021

"It is not because [humans] have made laws, that personality, liberty, and property exist. On the contrary, it is because personality, liberty, and property exist beforehand, that [humans] make laws."

- from the cover of The Law by Frédéric Bastiat [AMZN]

Lovely. No human gave us LIFE. No human gave us LIBERTY. No human gave us CHOICE. The law doesn't give us these things. Law is a human invention. And we invented laws to protect the things we are all born with.

CHOICE?

Frédéric calls your personal preference PERSONALITY. Makes sense. Your PERSONAL choices make up your PERSONAL-ITY. In the DOI (Declaration of Independence), Thomas decided to call your personal choices your HAPPINESS. I started using the word CHOICE. Which word would you use? Thomas had thought about making it self-evident that everyone has life, liberty, and PROPERTY, but he decided against saying PROPERTY, and instead said that you can choose to do whatever makes you happy. And that can include owning property. In THE UNITED STATES anyone can own property, even if you're not a citizen. This is not true for all nations. Even criminals can own property in the USA, including convicted murderers, rapists, and bankers.

DIDN'T YOUR PARENTS GIVE YOU LIFE?

Mommies and daddies didn't invent life to give it. No human invented life. Nothing on EARTH invented life. Nothing we can see with the eye has invented life.

Even squirrels are born with LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE

All beings are born with their LIFE, the LIBERTY to come and go, and their personal preference of nut. Life, liberty, and choice exist for all life with or without a human-ity. Life, liberty, and choice exist for all life with or without a squirrel-ity.

FREEDOM IS FREE

It's the protecting it that can cost lives and money. It doens't always need to be this way. One day if we work together, it can only cost money. We do need to build the ships and submarines and supply our armies to make sure that no one tries to come over here and infringe tyranny on us. Sure, we could always spend more or less to protect LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE. But the point is to spend less LIFE to protect LIFE. No human is expendable. Including those that protect us. The lives of our protective population are equal to the lives of everyone, and it is our duty to protect their lives, as it is their duty to protect ours.

More like the DECLARATION OF TRUTH

We HUMANS are all independent beings. Not becauses we made it so, but because it is the truth. Thomas said life, liberty, and choice were BIRTH GIVEN RIGHTS endowed by a creator. And whether or not you believe in a creator, the truth is that no human gave us these things. We all came out the womb with these BIRTH GIVEN TRUTHS.

We are all born EQUAL

Each and every human has equal capacity for LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE. Our different bodies, shapes, and sizes do not make us any less equal to one another. The human mind is what makes us equal. And human means wise being. And we are wise to understand that all humans, regardless of their body configuration, are all capable of being fully human.

The five parts of the DOI

"The declaration is not divided into formal sections; but it is often discussed as consisting of five parts: introduction, preamble, indictment of King George III, denunciation of the British people, and conclusion."

- from Wikipedia

Usually we talk about the constitution as the main document of THE UNITED STATES. But it is actually the truths expressed in the preamble of the DOI that established the unification of the thirteen states. The constitution only established a means of cooperation amongst the states, and this wasn't ratified until 1788, twelve years after the DOI. Being a scholar of the constitution is useful and will help you understand the intricacies of forming and operating a centralized governing entity. But being a scholar of the DOI will help you understand the truth of existence. We are all the same. No one better than another. No one in charge of another. No one to rule over another.

Infringe on our LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE and it's off with your head

In the DOI is states that if you try to take away our LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE, then we are willing to go to war with you. This is a matter of life or death. Sometimes we say GIVE ME LIBERTY OR GIVE ME DEATH. But it can't be given, only protected. Let's PROTECT LIBERTY, EVEN IN THE FACE OF DEATH. Let's PROTECT TRUTH. 'Nuff said.

It is your DUTY to uphold LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE

"...all experience hath shewn, that [humankind] are more disposed to suffer, while evils are sufferable, than to right themselves by abolishing the forms to which they are accustomed. But when a long train of abuses and usurpations, pursuing invariably the same Object evinces a design to reduce them under absolute Despotism, it is their right, it is their duty, to throw off such Government, and to provide new Guards for their future security."

- from the The United States Declaration of Independence, first drafted by Thomas Jefferson

Lovely. This is acknowledging that humans tend to take on suffering, but when it gets to be too much, you must stand up to this suffering. It is not your choice to protect LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE. It is your DUTY. Well, technically speaking, you can choose to not protect LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE. And I suppose that we the people of THESE UNITED STATES could choose to deport you. If any government tries to take away these truths of LIFE, LIBERTY, and CHOICE, you must stand up to this government. And thankfully there are many of us, so you won't be standing alone.

I'm PRO-LIFE, PRO-LIBERTY, and PRO-CHOICE

Right now the country is split. Half are PRO-LIFE and the rest are PRO-CHOICE, and I'm starting to question who here is actually PRO-LIBERTY.

TYRANNY

Thankfully here in the USA we are not under TYRANNY. Some people want to claim such, but it's not tyranny. Let's take a look at some of the indicments of tyranny straight from the DOI:
  1. He has erected a multitude of New Offices, and sent hither swarms of Officers to harass our people and eat out their substance. Bro, no one in the USA is coming to my home and eating my food. In fact, if Unlce Joe wants to come over for a burger, mi casa es su casa. Su casa es mi casa, también. I mean if I come to your home ever, Uncle Joey, I'm acting like it's my home, too. And it's your CHOICE to make my home yours. So if you enter my home and you're not looking around the fridge and eating food, then I'll shake my head at you until you eat at least one fish stick. And this goes for Uncle Donny and Daddy Barack, too. Look, I've got this frozen pizza with a cheese crust that I've been saving. It's got two and a half feet of cheese, get this, in the crust alone. It's really too many calories for me, but if we split it between the four of us, we'll each get at least 6 inches. And let me tell you, 6 inches is plenty. I've had longer, but you can feel it in your tummy, and it's a bit uncomfortable.
  2. He has kept among us, in times of peace, Standing Armies without the Consent of our legislatures. Yeah, no one is keeping armies standing around without consent of our legislature. They are standing around with full consent of our legislature. So maybe THE UNITED STATES MILITARY could come through every city for a parade. That way they're doing less standing and more parading. One of my favorite CHICAGO activities is the AIR AND WATER SHOW. Really my favorite part is the week before when they're practicing and flying all over the city. It feels like being in a war minus the bombs and constant fear of death. So it's pretty fun.
  3. He has called together legislative bodies at places unusual, uncomfortable, and distant from the depository of their Public Records, for the sole purpose of fatiguing them into compliance with his measures. Bro, a roundtrip flight on SouthWest is like 100 bucks on sale. And there are multiple flights to DC daily from around the nation. We could put the capital in ALASKA and everyone could make it up there no problem. In fact, there is a ton of cheap land in ALASKA, let's build a secondary capital there. That way it's closer to RUSSIA for strategic purposes. They'll be shaking in their boots once the president moves to ALASKA.

We don't live under TYRANNY in THE UNITED STATES

Maybe you don't like what our leaders are doing. Maybe there is some TOMFOOLERY. But it's not TYRANNY. The only tyranny I've witnessed in THE UNITED STATES was at the hands of parents, teachers, and the DMV.

Here in THESE UNITED STATES, the only thing we love as much as FREEDOM is outdoor BBQs

Here's what I'm thinking. July is a pretty warm month. And everyone likes being out and about. We got the 4th as a holiday, how about doing the 11th as a holiday, too? Think about it. Two four-day work weeks to start off the month of July. Doesn't that sound AMAZING? What's the point of protecting our freedom if we're not going to use it to BBQ as much as possible? The 250th anniversary of the DOI is coming up in 2026, and a week later on the 11th of July we could have a a big convention where we sign a new document called THE DECLARATION OF TRUTH. We're not replacing the DOI, we're just creating an additional, supplementary document that will be used to foster vacations, I mean freedom.

Doesn't that look like fun? Who wants to have some fun?

July 11th, 2026 could be THE UNITED STATES DECLARATION OF TRUTH CONVENTION. And it's on a Saturday, for maximum barbequing. Here's who I'm suggesting be at this convention: - current and past presidents - Stephen Kevin B. - ??? - FREEDOM I figure if you can get the presidents plus STEVE BANNON to sign this DECLARATION OF TRUTH, then you really have something special. And if you haven't seen that FRONTLINE interview, get ready for a civics lesson. Love him or hate him, Steve understands what he's talking about, is thoughtful, and he's also ready to engage in discourse, as every citizen of THE UNITED STATES should be.

Everyone can wear white wigs and paint their faces white, like clowns. Red noses optional. Whether you're in WHITE FACE or BLACK FACE, one thing is for sure, you're a clown. Only clowns put on makeup and wear elevation shoes before going to work in the mornings. Well, only clowns, entertainers, and humans that were lead to believe that they can only be beautiful if in makeup. I used to think humans in makeup were beautiful. I still think they're beautiful, but now I also think they're clowns. And I'm here to tell you that you're beautiful, with or without CLOWN FACE.

We already have INDEPENDENCE DAY. After the convention we can add TRUTH DAY. Who likes the sound of that?

USS TRUTH

We built the USS INDEPENDENCE. How about building the USS TRUTH? Who likes the sound of that?

Right now when you hear the name MOHAMMAD, maybe you think of NINE-ELEVEN. And I was hoping that instead you would start thinking of SEVEN-ELEVEN.
