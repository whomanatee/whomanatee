PEACE PROSPERITY JOY

This global institution is a framework designed to unify global resources, services, and populations under one integrated system to promote efficiency, cooperation, and sustainability.

It aims to replace traditional governments, NGOs, and separate international entities by providing all essential services—including education, housing, and healthcare—through collective efforts and advanced technologies, ensuring that every individual can live comfortably by contributing just two days of work per week.
