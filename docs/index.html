WORLD PEACE PROSPERITY JOY

This global institution is a framework designed to unify global resources, services, and populations under one integrated system to promote efficiency and cooperation.

The institution aims to replace traditional governments, NGOs, and separate international entities by providing all essential services—including education, housing, and healthcare—through collective efforts and advanced technologies, ensuring that every individual can live comfortably by contributing on average just two days of work per week.

The global institution fervently champions creativity, empowering every person to discover and enhance their creative talents, fostering an environment where innovation and artistic expression flourish.

Embracing inclusivity, the institution ensures that all, including those with troubled pasts or in prison, regardless of age, ability, background, or location, is actively included and valued, guaranteeing that no one is forgotten and every life is empowered.
