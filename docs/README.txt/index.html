============== README.txt ============== This institution is committed to going with only the best ideas to create an incredible world for all. In order to find the best ideas, everyone will need to be comfortable arguing/persuading without anger or fear in front of a room full of people of any size. To help get ready to argue in front of others, below are a few resources that you can peruse at your own pace: -------------- 1. For this writing by Jay that he called Thank You For Arguing, peruse the intro, peruse the first chapter, look through the appendices, and peruse quotes at the beginnings of chapters. Any edition. 2. For this writing by Aristotle that he called Rhetoric, peruse the first section of book one. 3. For this writing by John that he called Lectures on Oratory, peruse the table of contents.
